# Product Hunt launch kit

PH is the one *spike* channel in this set — a decent launch is 300–1500 site
visits in 24h and a permanent listing that ranks in Google. Launch ONCE, when
ready; you can't meaningfully re-launch the same product.

## When to launch (gate, don't rush)

Launch only after ALL of these are true, or the spike is wasted:
- [ ] GoatCounter is live (so the spike is measurable)
- [ ] A 30–60s demo video exists (listings with video convert ~2x)
- [ ] `brew install` one-liner is live and on the website
- [ ] You have the day free — makers who answer every comment all day rank far higher

**Best slot:** Tuesday or Wednesday, **12:01am PT** (the daily leaderboard
resets then; launching at 12:01 gives a full 24h to accumulate votes).

## Day-of playbook

- Launch 12:01am PT; first comment up by 12:05.
- Reply to **every** comment within minutes, all day — comment velocity is PH's
  main ranking signal.
- Personally message friends a link saying "I launched today, would love your
  honest feedback" — PH penalizes "please upvote" solicitation, so never use
  those words, and never share a direct-to-upvote link.
- Post the launch on X/LinkedIn in the morning windows (agent will have drafts
  ready — tell it the launch date the day before via a note in the repo).
- Add a "🎉 We're on Product Hunt today" banner/link on the website for the day.
