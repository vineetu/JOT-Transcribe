**Description:**
Jot is a dictation utility for the Mac menu bar. Press a global hotkey, speak,
and the transcript is pasted at your cursor in whatever app you're using.

Everything important happens on-device: transcription runs on the Apple Neural
Engine and audio never leaves your Mac. No account, no subscription, no
telemetry. Open source under the MIT license.

Highlights:
- Paste-at-cursor dictation in any app via global hotkey
- 100% on-device transcription (Parakeet on the Neural Engine), works offline
- Rewrite selected text with a spoken instruction
- Optional AI cleanup — Apple Intelligence on-device by default; Ollama or
  your own API keys if you prefer
- Custom vocabulary for names and jargon
- Searchable history of past dictations
- Auto-updates via Sparkle
